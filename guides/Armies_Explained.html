What Are Armies?

Club Penguin armies are an age-old culture in the Club Penguin world. They started off as gangs on the original Club Penguin. However, they emerged as armies in 2006. One of the first armies include the Army of Club Penguin, the Special Weapons and Tactics, and the Doritos of Club Penguin.
