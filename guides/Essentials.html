Glossary

Recruiting is the process of sending an invitation to an individual, asking them to join SWAT.

Mentoring is the process of requesting a recruit to enlist, and also to welcome and interact with them. Remember, enlistment is only considered if the recruit creates an account on CPA Battleground.

Attending is the act of participating in an event.

These three essential elements combine to form the important activities you are expected to follow in SWAT. If these three are followed consistently, you will become strong enough to keep your position. Please refer to the guides of the three essential chores to learn more about them.
