Diplomacy

In the realm of Club Penguin armies, diplomacy is an important topic. Diplomacy is the activity of curating external affairs and relations.

Etiquette In Other Armies

When present in another army as a staff member, you should not bring in irrelevant or unnecessary drama. Avoid breaking rules in Discord servers of other armies. As a staff member in SWAT, you represent a face of the army. Armies to which SWAT is neutral or allied, should see more disciplined diplomacy. Your behavior may have some influence on a future international negotiation. This advice is not limited to army servers solely, but also army communities elsewhere, including the Club Penguin Armies Discord.

Know Your Diplomacy

You should have sufficient knowledge about SWAT's diplomacy with other armies. Having some knowledge about the armies SWAT is allied with can help, as well as knowing SWAT's enemy armies. You can view diplomatic information on the SWAT website's Empire page.

You can also view the Club Penguin Armies (CPA) league map on CPA website's Map page. It maps armies on the map that possess land.

Staying Tuned

It is very helpful to stay tuned to latest news around the army community. Staying updated helps you learn much more about the army community and its past. Living under the rock might be a little difficult, as Club Penguin armies is hard to grasp. With your knowledge of what is happening around the army society, you will be able to make better decisions and learn leadership skills. You can gain knowledge by reading articles on the Club Penguin Armies (CPA) league website.
