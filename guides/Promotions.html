Promotions

Promotions are incentives rewarded to an enlisted SWAT troop for attending an event.

Notes

  • Ideally, promotions of an event should be completed in no more than 24 hours after the event. It is encouraged to complete promotions immediately after an event.
  • Promotions of an event should not be done in less than 30 minutes after an event ends, unless you are confident that all attendees have submitted their names.
  • Everyone, including staff members, should submit their name in promotions, even if they are not willing to receive a promotion. We have to track event attendance in our records internally.
COMPLETION

All promotions should be completed before the month ends. This is because seasons need to continue, and a winner has to be determined for a season. The winner cannot be determined if promotions of all the events of the past month have not been finalized.
