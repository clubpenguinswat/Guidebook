Essentials: Attending Events

Attendance at events is necessary. You must also not be passive at events, and actively participate. Attending an event comes with some important notes that should be kept in mind.

Vision

In Club Penguin, army events have a vision, which lays the foundation of the concept of armies on this game. The goal of an army is to dominate a server by being its boss. To do so, an army often battles another army for land (or "server"). An army has to fight the other army using tactics. The army that is the fastest in sending tactics, and is the most noticeable, wins.
