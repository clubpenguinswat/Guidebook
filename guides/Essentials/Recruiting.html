Essentials: Recruiting

Recruiting is the process of inviting an individual into the SWAT Discord server. Visitors do not count towards recruiting. A person who recruits is known as a recruiter, and an individual being recruited is known as a recruitee.

Methods

There are multiple methods to recruit. You should always prefer a method that you feel you can bring the most recruits in, or you may try as many methods as possible. The methods have been listed below…

  • Discord scouting: You may approach a few members in another Discord server, asking them if they are willing to join SWAT. Various approaches are followed for the purpose of recruiting. For example, you may use a hands-on approach and play a game with them, then ask them if they are interested in joining a server. Or, you may directly as them if they are willing to join SWAT. The choice of how you wish to scout is yours. Note that different approaches and different sources give you different quality of recruits.
  • Friends and family: You may ask your friends or family members if they are interested in joining SWAT.
  • Adverts: Setting up adverts on various places is a rare activity done for recruiting members into SWAT. While it is not very popular and effective, it has been effective in getting some key SWAT players in the past.

Discord Scouting Explained

Discord scouting is a recommended recruiting method, and is widely used in the entire army community. It is very important to learn how to scout on Discord. However, you should create a Discord alternate account [alt] first. Alternatively, you can also request for a Discord recruitment profile.

DISCORD SCOUTING

For Discord scouting, we encourage you to use an alternate account [alt] on Discord. This is because you be charged penalties, including a ban, from a Discord server you may be recruiting in. Using another account secures you these penalties on your main account.

Creating an account

You can create an account on Discord, which you can utilize for Discord scouting. To achieve this, you will need to create an email address first. Creating a Google account works, but may be a lengthy process. You can quickly create an email account using Proton Mail. After creating your account, set up a decent profile. It is not necessary for it to be attractive, but pick a casual and safe theme.

The MINECRAFT Discord server is one of the largest on the platform. You may not receive penalties if you advertise via DMs in the server! Thus, these features make it a good target for recruiters. Join the MINECRAFT server.

Messaging

To begin scouting on Discord, open a Discord server you wish to use for recruiting. Then, visit their main chat and look for active users. Select a user you have not messaged before and send your recruiting message in their DM. A few messages you may use for recruiting have been described in the next section in this article. However, before you send the message, make sure to check their mutual servers and ensure they are not already in another Club Penguin army Discord server.

TROOPSTEALING

When recruiting, you should ensure that the person you are trying to recruit is not already in a Club Penguin army. We encourage you to join army Discord servers on your recruiting account and let them know that you are a recruiting account sent from SWAT, joining to avoid accidentally troopstealing. Then, keep an eye on an the mutual servers you share with an individual you are recruiting. Do not recruit a member who is already present in a Club Penguin army Discord server.

You can find a list of army servers in the armies channel of the CPA Battleground server.
